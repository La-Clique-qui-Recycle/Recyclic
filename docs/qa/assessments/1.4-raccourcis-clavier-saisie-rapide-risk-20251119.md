## Detailed Risk Analysis

### Medium Risk

**RISK-001: Focus Workflow Complexity**
- **Description**: Complex automatic focus management between categories → weight → categories could lead to user confusion or unexpected behavior
- **Probability**: Medium (complex interaction patterns)
- **Impact**: Medium (could disrupt user workflow if focus transitions are not smooth)
- **Mitigation**: Comprehensive testing of focus transitions, clear visual indicators, user testing in staging
- **Status**: ⚠️ Monitored - Extensive integration tests implemented, but requires production monitoring

### Low Risk

**RISK-002: Dual Implementation Maintenance**
- **Description**: Maintaining two separate shortcut implementations (database-driven vs position-based) increases maintenance complexity
- **Probability**: Low (well-documented and tested)
- **Impact**: Low (separate concerns, clear separation of implementations)
- **Mitigation**: Clear documentation, comprehensive test coverage, modular design
- **Status**: ℹ️ Accepted - Acceptable complexity given the architectural benefits
