## Detailed Risk Analysis

### Low Risk

**RISK-001: Focus-Based Approach Limitations**
- **Description**: Visual borders depend on focus location rather than explicit step state, which may be less predictable for some users
- **Probability**: Low (focus-based approach is intuitive and matches natural user behavior)
- **Impact**: Low (borders still provide clear visual feedback, just based on focus rather than step state)
- **Mitigation**: Focus-based approach actually provides more immediate and relevant feedback
- **Status**: ℹ️ Accepted - Focus-based approach is actually more user-friendly than step-state approach
